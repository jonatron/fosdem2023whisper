The talk is about using CICD workflows for documentation, with a focus on automating the process and adhering to a consistent style guide. The speaker covers the problems that arise in documentation, the importance of voice and tone, and provides some examples of style guides used by companies like Google and Microsoft. The talk also includes a demo on using GitHub Actions, Markdown, and Veil to automate the documentation process. The benefits of automation are outlined, but the speaker also cautions that proper team communication and avoiding false positives are essential.
