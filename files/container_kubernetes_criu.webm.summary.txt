In this talk, Adrian Reber discusses the integration of Kubernetes and Checkpoint Restore using the tool CRIU. He provides background information on Checkpoint Restore and its integration into different container engines. Reber presents various use cases for container migration and highlights technical details about how CRIU works. He also talks about the future of Checkpoint Restore in Kubernetes, including the potential for kubectl checkpoint and kubectl migrate commands. Reber concludes by addressing questions from the audience.
