The talk discussed the W3C organization and its role in defining the web RTC standard, which was only released in 2021. The working group focuses on various topics such as optimization for better video quality and improving user experience. Anyone can contribute to the working group, and it encourages the participation of individuals, organizations, and companies. The talk provided information on how to engage with the group through its website, monthly discussions, group repositories, and events. The speaker also discussed the possibility of proposing new topics, such as energy consumption and decentralized messaging.
