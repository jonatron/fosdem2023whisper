In this talk, the speaker discussed using Janus for implementing social audio applications that are mainly audio-based, like Twitter Spaces and Clubhouse. He described the challenges involved and potential solutions such as using the Janus AudioBridge plugin and streaming plugin to mix the audio, RTP Forwarding, and Multicast distribution. He also discussed how rules, such as those determining presenters, can be implemented at an application level.
