The speaker, Gary Niel from Antibaba Cloud, presented on enabling NIDAS image service for confidential containers at the FOSDEM conference. He discussed the special requirements for image management for confidential containers, the current available technologies, and the challenges they still face. He then introduced the NIDAS image project, discussing its design, features, and current status. In the end, he presented his ideas to enhance NIDAS image service to improve image loading processes for confidential containers, including adding data encryption and integrity verification. The speaker also discussed their development plans to integrate the NIDAS image service with the image IIS create and enabling access through URFS.
