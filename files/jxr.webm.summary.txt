The speaker gave a talk at the FOSDEM conference in 2023 about their experience programming in virtual reality (VR) on a plane. They discussed their motivation behind exploring VR as an interface for programming and how they developed their own programming language using JavaScript. The speaker demonstrated different features of their VR environment, such as manipulating text and executing code, organizing code blocks, and visualizing 3D models and graphs. They also highlighted the potential of VR as a learning tool in various fields. The speaker acknowledged their use of VR headsets from Meta (formerly Facebook), but emphasized the importance of exploring alternatives and not relying on proprietary platforms. The talk concluded with a suggestion to build personalized interfaces and scaffolding for programming, whether using VR or not.
