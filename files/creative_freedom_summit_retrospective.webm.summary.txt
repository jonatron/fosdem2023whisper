The talk is about the Creative Freedom Summit, a virtual event that was focused on promoting open-source creative software, spreading knowledge of how to use them, and connecting creatives across the FOSS ecosystem. It was hosted by Emma and Jess, who are both part of the Fedora design team. The summit had 18 speakers, with an average of 50 viewers per session, and over 700 unique viewers spread out across 54 different countries. The talk discusses what went well and what could be improved for next year's summit. The primary goals of the summit were to promote open-source creative software, spreading knowledge of how to use it, and connecting creatives across the FOSS ecosystem. The talk also covers the setup for the summit, the platforms used, and how to participate in Fedora. The speakers also answer questions about the summit and potential improvements for next year.
