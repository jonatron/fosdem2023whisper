
 
This talk explored the opportunities and possibilities of using alternative virtual platforms to host digital events in the wake of Covid-19. It covered a range of ideas, such as offering synchronous Q&A sessions and Slack channels for post-event discussions, and recommended the use of platforms such as Hopin and Zoom for online events. The speaker also discussed how these virtual platforms can work in sync with in-person events, as well as different ways of providing content to global audiences, such as pre-recording events.
