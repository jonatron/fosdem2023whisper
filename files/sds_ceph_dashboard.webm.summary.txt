The talk given at the FOSDEM conference in 2023 was about the Ceph Dashboard, which is a management and monitoring web UI tool for the Ceph storage system. The speaker, Nizamuddin, discussed the history, architecture, and key features of the dashboard. They also explained why the dashboard is not just limited to monitoring and emphasized its ability to handle management functionalities. The speaker showcased a demo of the dashboard and discussed the future roadmap, including plans for the Reef release. In the second part of the talk, Ankush highlighted how users can contribute to the Ceph Dashboard as users, translators, documenters, or developers. They also mentioned the Ceph Dashboard community and shared some details about their meetings and face-to-face events.
