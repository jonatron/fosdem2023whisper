

This talk introduced the attendees to matrix and provided some stats about its successful growth since 2015. Matrix is an open network for secure and decentralized real-time communication, offering chat, voiceover IP, IOT, VR, 3D worlds and more. It has 87 million known users and 13-14 million users across at least 100,000 deployments, ranging from Raspberry Pis to large-scale projects. It is backed by open source projects, governments, companies, and has over 4,000 committers and 50,000 stars on its GitHub repo.
