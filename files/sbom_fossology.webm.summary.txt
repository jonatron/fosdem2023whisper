

In this talk, Shaheem and Gaurav from Siemens presented on the open source license compliance project, Phosology. It scans for licenses, copyrights, authorships, emails, ECC statements, keywords and IP address statements. It also produces documentation such as readme OSS text documentation and unified reports. Additionally, they discussed recent releases and features, such as integration with Scan Code, SBOM support and GDPR updates. They are also planning to move to React, reduce false positives and ensure their source code is reused.software standard compliant.
