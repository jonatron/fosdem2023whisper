In this talk, Olivier Caix gives an update on the latest developments in the G-Streamer community. He discusses major releases, the growing investment in Rust modules, and improvements to the build system. He also highlights advancements in areas such as WebRTC, RTP error correction, RTP extension support, video decoding, HLS and DASH base classes, machine learning integration, UI integration with GTK4 and QT6, and new tracers. Q&A from the audience includes questions on SVC support in RTP AV1, congestion control in WebRTC, the benefits of using WebRTC sync for custom signaling, and QT6 direct 3D integration.
