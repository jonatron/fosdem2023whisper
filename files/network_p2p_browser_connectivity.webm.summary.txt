 

In this talk, the speaker introduced the LiPi2P project, a peer-to-peer networking library with one specification implemented in many languages. The speaker then discussed the various ways to establish connection between a peer and a browser, by means of connection reversal and hole punching where needed, as well as the use of web sockets, web transport, and WebRTC. The talk concluded by informing the audience of the usage and available resources of LiPi2P.
