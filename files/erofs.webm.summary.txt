

In his talk, Xiang Gao presented URF5, Enhanced Read-Only File System, as a file system with high performance for a variety of applications, including Android system partitions and container images. He discussed the kernel-mountable characteristics and the features of URF5 such as the core Alv4 and LVMA transparent data compression, and the latest features of the system such as chunk-based files, multiple devices and blocks, data deduplicated encryption solutions, and verification solutions.
