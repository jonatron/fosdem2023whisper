

In this talk, Sara Krisch discusses open source software development in the context of AppStream collaboration, Linux distribution collaboration, and Mainframe systems. She explains how the OpenMainframe project was founded in 2015 and its key focus points, why the Linux distribution working group was created, and how upstream projects are being included in collaboration efforts. She outlines how the collaboration benefits all involved, reducing research and development costs and providing a platform for innovation. She also explains the process of submitting bugs and issues, and how open source projects can join the Linux distribution working group.
