

In this talk, Mark discussed the importance of rotating secrets in order to ensure the security of your systems and to comply with industry standards. He also dug into the "secret rotation" process and showed how it works in the context of Kubernetes and third-party secret stores such as AWS, Hashicorp's Vault, and GitHub. Mark also demonstrated how to achieve automated secret rotation with the help of tools like External Secrets and Reloader and how this process works in tandem with synchronized Kubernetes secrets and config maps.
