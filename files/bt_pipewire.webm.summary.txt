

In this talk, Frédéric Danisse discussed the use of PipeWire and WirePobler for Bluetooth test in wire pramber and per-pramber, with an emphasis on low latency and gravity processing engine audio and video streams. He covered Bluetooth classic audio profiles like SBC and AAC codecs, Advanced Audio Distribution Profiles, and HFP specs, as well as future-facing low-energy audio specs like IC3 codecs, Isoconus radio channels, and Holocaust broadcast mode.
