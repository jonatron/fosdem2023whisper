The speaker discusses the FUJI project, which is aimed at bringing all users of OpenJDK together to have one place to be updated about Java releases, including Kotlin releases. The project offers a website called FUJIO, with information on new releases, fixes, and enhancements. The project also has a Slack channel for people to join and discuss Java-related topics. Throughout the day, there will be a range of different people from different companies talking about various Java-related topics every 20 minutes. The speaker encourages people to visit the FUJIO website and join the Slack channel.
