In this talk, the speaker discussed their product, FedExcess, which is a software that helps workshop managers switch on and off machines while also providing data on which machines were used and which users used them. The speaker discussed their approach, which is modular and designed to be easy for both the workshop manager and the users, as well as technical details such as the use of Rust, Captain Proto, and an MQTT server. The speaker also mentioned the community of developers who are helping build and improve FedExcess.
