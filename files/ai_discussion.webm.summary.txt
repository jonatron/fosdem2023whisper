The talk at the FOSDEM conference discussed the topic of AI and its implications for the free and open source software development community. Participants raised questions and shared their thoughts on various aspects, such as the ethics of AI, regulations in different regions, the need for ethics training for mathematicians in AI, the need for explainability in AI models, the challenges of competing with closed models, the role of licenses in regulating AI, and the concept of AI as legal persons. While some participants emphasized the importance of openness and replicability in AI models, others expressed concerns about the misuse of AI and the need for regulations. Overall, the discussion highlighted the complexities and open questions surrounding AI in the context of free and open source software development.
