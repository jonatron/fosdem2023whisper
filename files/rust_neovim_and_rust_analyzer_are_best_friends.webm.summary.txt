 

In this talk Andrei discussed the highly popular language and editor combination of Rust and NeoVim. He discussed the extensibility, usability, and added ability of NeoVim to write plugins in Lua, as well as how NeoVim is capable of working as a client to LSP (Language Server Protocol) servers such as Rust Analyzer. Andrei also went through other features, such as the Mason plugin to install language servers, telescope key maps, LSP references, and the capabilities of Rust Analyzer.
