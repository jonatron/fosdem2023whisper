In this talk given at the FOSDEM conference in 2023, Edward discusses the tools he has built for adding links between OpenStreetMap and Wikidata. He explains the differences between the two systems, the types of objects and tags they use, and the benefits of linking them. Edward also demonstrates his software, which allows users to search for matches between OpenStreetMap and Wikidata and add links between the two systems. He mentions the limitations of the software, such as the inability to handle tunnels and rivers in OpenStreetMap, and requests help from the community to further develop the tool. Edward also answers questions about the software, its licenses, and future improvements. Overall, the talk highlights the importance of linking OpenStreetMap and Wikidata, and showcases the software Edward has built to facilitate this process.
