

Oliver gave a presentation on ONDEV2, a free and open source installer that comes pre-installed with the post marketer community edition and is also used by Mobian. He detailed the various features of the installer, including full disk encryption, the ability to choose the file system, the language and locale, as well as a simple or advanced installation option. He explained the testing process and tools used to ensure the installer works correctly, as well as plans to make it distro independent and potentially expand it to other devices and platforms.
