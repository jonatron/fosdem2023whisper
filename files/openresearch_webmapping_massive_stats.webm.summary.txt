This talk recounted the progress of a project of web mapping and statistical data eight years ago, culminating in today's current tools which allow for efficient visualization of data, allowing people to explore data on the web with ease. The talk highlighted the importance of vector tiles, details the aggregation of data, and addressed the issues with the Modifiable Area Unit Problem. It also mentioned some European initiatives which have been implemented to help with the visualization of statistical data.
