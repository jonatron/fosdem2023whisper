The talk was a panel discussion at the FOSDEM conference in 2023 about legal and policy issues related to free and open source software development. The panelists discussed various topics including the positive changes in attitudes towards free software, the importance of software freedom in today's world, the need for transparency in AI and cybersecurity, the challenges of enforcing copyleft licenses, and the potential for public money to support open source initiatives. They also talked about the need for collaboration and engagement with other fields like open data and open hardware. The panelists emphasized the importance of reaching out to other communities and building bridges to advance the cause of free software.
