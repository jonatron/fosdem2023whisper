The speaker discusses FAIRpoints, an event series that highlights automatic measures developed by the community towards the implementation of the FAIR data principles. The talk explains the four principles of FAIR (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, and Reusable) and the difficulty of understanding how they translate to real-life solutions. FAIRpoints offers a platform for conversations around creating realistic and pragmatic FAIR implementations. This includes community discussions, keynote events, and Ask Me Anything events. The talk emphasizes the importance of including diverse voices and creating a global community around FAIR. The Q&A section addresses questions about machine actionability and working with diverse research communities. The speaker provides links to the Fairpoints website, event series, Slack, and newsletter for those interested in learning more.
