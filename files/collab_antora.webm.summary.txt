The talk was about creating a content pipeline with Intora, a free and open source software development tool. The speakers discussed how Intora can be used to gather content from multiple sources and automate the process of creating content for multiple websites and downstream processes. They highlighted the benefits of using Intora in an upstream-downstream model for documentation, including better documentation, faster content development, and improved time to market. They also mentioned the use of Antora extensions, such as Antora Collector and Antora Assembler, to enhance the content creation process. They concluded by showcasing a demo project that demonstrated how Intora and its extensions can be used to simplify and automate content management and collaboration.
