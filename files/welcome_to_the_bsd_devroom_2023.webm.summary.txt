The talk was an introduction and brief announcement of the schedule for the morning session at the FOSDEM conference in 2023. The speaker emphasized the rules and protocols set in place, such as cleaning up after oneself and not bringing food inside the room, to maintain cleanliness and orderliness. The speaker also reminded attendees that masks were not mandatory but encouraged, and that the room would be ventilated after each talk. The schedule was briefly presented, with the first talk on BSD drivers and the last talk on Chimera Linux.
