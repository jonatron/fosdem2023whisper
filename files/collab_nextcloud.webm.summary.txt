In this talk at the FOSDEM conference, Joss, co-founder of NexCloud, discusses the new features and updates in NexCloud. He highlights the introduction of user migration, file locking, improved performance, and various enhancements in NexCloud Hub 2. Joss also mentions the improvements in the Files app, Talk app, Groupware app, and Office app. He talks about the ease of use, scalability, and compatibility of NexCloud, including its availability on various platforms and its support for collaborative editing and federation. The audience asks questions about markdown support in the knowledge base, federation in Talk, sharing admin access in the forms app, and the capacity of video conferencing in Talk. Joss emphasizes that NexCloud is self-hosted, open source, and offers control over data privacy.
