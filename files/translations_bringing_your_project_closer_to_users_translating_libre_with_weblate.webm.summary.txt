The talk is about a software called Weblate, which is a free and open-source localization platform. Weblate aims to make the process of localization and coordination easier for developers, translators, and managers. It supports various file formats and integrates with version control systems. The speaker discusses the features and benefits of using Weblate, as well as future plans for the software, such as improving enterprise readiness and introducing universal search hints. The speaker also mentions different ways to support Weblate, including hosting services, custom development, and donations. Finally, there is a brief mention of the history and origin of Weblate, as well as the team behind it.
