The speaker discusses the Kairos Linux meta-distribution designed for edge computing, offering immutability, easy configuration, and distribution agnosticism. They explain the challenges faced when building Kairos, such as differing package names and the use of different init systems across distributions. The Kairos factory is introduced as the solution to building and maintaining Kairos images, which are read-only, immutable, and include special agents and Kubernetes packages. The speaker invites users to try Kairos, and concludes by offering stickers and a Q&A session.
