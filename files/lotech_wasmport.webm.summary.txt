

In this talk, the presenter spoke about Walmux, a Java extension for LibreOffice which was developed by the city of Munich. It is predominantly used in the public sector and provides a powerful platform for generating documents, forms, mail merges, and more. The codebase has recently been moved to CDF where documentation, bug fixes and UI adjustments are still being made. The presenter also invited participants to help with comment translations, UI translations, features and bug fixes.
